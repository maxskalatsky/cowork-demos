About the Ride

Community, Movement & Impact

WheELS for Autism is a purpose-driven cycling event created to bring together community, movement, inclusion, and impact.


This ride was started in 2024 by Max Skalatsky and a group of friends who came together to support Els for Autism through a meaningful cycling fundraiser. During the first ride, the group raised over $25,000 in support of Els for Autism and the life-changing work they do for individuals with autism and their families.

100% of donations raised through this ride will directly support Els for Autism and the life-changing services, programs, education, and resources they provide for individuals with autism and their families.
