Our Mission & Vision

Empowering seniors and individuals with disabilities to live with dignity, confidence, and independence through fierce advocacy and compassionate support.

Zen Senior Care Services is a proud 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.

How We Serve Our Community

🩺
Medical Advocacy & Healthcare
  • Coordinate communication between patients, families, and healthcare providers.
  • Explain diagnoses, treatment options, insurance benefits, and care plans.
  • Support hospital discharges, care transitions, and follow-up services.
  • Coordinate referrals and connections to healthcare providers and support services.
  • Connect individuals with community resources, caregiver support, and financial assistance.
🏠
Housing Stability & Advocacy
  • Connect individuals with safe, accessible, and affordable housing solutions.
  • Protect housing rights and advocate against evictions or displacement.
  • Navigate assisted living placements and long-term care options with confidence.
👨‍👩‍👧
Family & Caregiver Support
  • Educate families on Medicare, Medicaid, estate planning, and available benefits.
  • Provide one-on-one guidance through complex healthcare and financial decisions.
  • Offer peer support groups and resources to prevent caregiver burnout.
💚
Community & Well-being
  • Foster meaningful social engagement to combat isolation and loneliness.
  • Connect seniors with volunteer companions, events, and transportation services.
  • Link individuals with community resources, referrals, and financial assistance.

Meet Our Leadership Team

Nara Murillo
President

Nara Murillo is a dedicated nurse, real estate investor and passionate senior advocate committed to improving the lives of aging adults. With years of experience as a nurse case manager, she has a deep understanding of the complexities of healthcare systems and the unique needs of the senior population.

Beyond direct patient care, Nara has leveraged her healthcare expertise to become a successful real estate investor, specializing in properties that enhance the quality of life for seniors. As President of Zen Senior Care Services, she leads the organization with a mission-driven focus — helping seniors navigate the dual challenges of healthcare access and housing stability.

Under her leadership, Zen Senior Care Services tirelessly works to empower seniors, providing them with critical resources, advocacy, and support to live independently and comfortably. She is dedicated to connecting aging adults with essential services and safe, affordable housing solutions, ensuring that they receive the care and security they deserve.

Driven by a passion for impact, Nara seamlessly blends her clinical knowledge, business acumen, and nonprofit leadership to create innovative, lasting solutions in both healthcare and senior housing. Her work continues to transform lives by bridging gaps in care and housing, fostering independence, and championing dignity for older adults.

Barbara Slingluff
Chief Operating Officer

As Chief Operating Officer at Zen Senior Care Services, Barbara is dedicated to enhancing the safety, well-being, and quality of care for seniors. With extensive expertise in case management, utilization management (UM), Medicare, Medicaid, durable medical equipment (DME), and transitions of care, she plays a pivotal role in ensuring seniors receive holistic, compassionate, and secure care at every stage of their healthcare journey.

Barbara is a registered nurse with hands-on experience in cardiac telemetry and emergency medicine, giving her a deep understanding of the medical complexities and safety concerns affecting older adults. Her unique perspective fuels her passion for creating healthcare environments where seniors thrive with confidence and dignity.

Throughout her career, Barbara has led transformative initiatives that prioritize both safety and quality of care. She spearheaded the Post-Acute Council and Hospital Transportation Program, ensuring high-risk seniors have safe and reliable access to medical appointments. Additionally, she developed the Post-Hospital Discharge Clinic (PHDC) — a critical support system designed to mitigate hospital readmissions, conduct essential safety checks, and provide tailored recovery care.

Her commitment to senior care innovation continues to drive improved healthcare delivery for vulnerable seniors navigating complex medical systems, making a lasting impact on their health, safety, and overall well-being.
